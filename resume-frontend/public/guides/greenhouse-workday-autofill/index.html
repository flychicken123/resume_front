How to auto-fill Greenhouse and Workday job applications?

Auto-fill Greenhouse & Workday applications instantly

Greenhouse and Workday applications are long and repetitive. HiHired Auto-Fill detects every field — name, email, work history, education, EEO — and fills them automatically in under 3 seconds.

Install the HiHired Chrome extension and navigate to any Greenhouse or Workday job application. Click the extension icon, and it fills all fields from your saved resume data. It handles multi-page forms, dropdown selects, React components, and EEO fields automatically.

Step-by-step guide

  1. 1. Install HiHired Auto-Fill

    Free Chrome extension from the Chrome Web Store.

  2. 2. Set up your resume

    Build or import your resume at hihired.org — your data is saved for auto-fill.

  3. 3. Open a Greenhouse/Workday application

    Navigate to any job posting on boards.greenhouse.io, *greenhouse.io, or workday.com.

  4. 4. Click Fill Application

    The extension detects all fields and fills them from your resume in under 3 seconds.

  5. 5. Handle special fields

    EEO fields (race, gender, veteran status), work authorization, and custom questions are filled too.

  6. 6. Review and submit

    Quick review, then submit. What used to take 15 minutes now takes 30 seconds.
