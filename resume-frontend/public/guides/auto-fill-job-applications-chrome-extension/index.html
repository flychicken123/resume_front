How do I auto-fill job applications with a Chrome extension?

Auto-fill job applications in one click

The HiHired Chrome extension reads your saved resume and fills every field on a job application page automatically — name, email, phone, work history, education, location, and EEO fields — in under 3 seconds.

Install the HiHired Auto-Fill Chrome extension, log in with your hihired.org account, and click the extension icon on any job application page. It detects all form fields and fills them from your resume instantly. Works on Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, LinkedIn Easy Apply, iCIMS, SmartRecruiters, and 100+ other platforms.

Step-by-step guide

  1. 1. Install the extension

    Add HiHired Auto-Fill from the Chrome Web Store (free).

  2. 2. Build your resume

    Create or import your resume at hihired.org/builder — takes 4 minutes.

  3. 3. Navigate to a job

    Open any job application page on Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, LinkedIn, or any company career site.

  4. 4. Click Fill Application

    Click the HiHired icon in your toolbar, then hit Fill Application. All fields fill instantly.

  5. 5. Review and submit

    Check the auto-filled fields, make any edits, and submit your application.
